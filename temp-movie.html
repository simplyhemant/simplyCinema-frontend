Synopsis

When the world is overrun by darkness and the tyrannical ruler Kali dominates the surviving city of Kasi, a modern-day avatar of Vishnu descends to Earth. A bounty hunter named Bhairava and a group of rebels embark on a perilous journey to protect the woman carrying the child who will end the age of darkness. A grand cinematic saga blending ancient mythology with cutting-edge science fiction.
